Synopsis: 
    todoish.sh [add|tasks|remove|show-completed]

Description:   
    Todoish is a command-line tool designed for programmers to manage their tasks directly in the terminal. 
    It provides a powerful scripting environment for managing tasks without needing to switch between different 
    contexts or third-party apps. Whether you are in a meeting or have tasks that need to be done, you can easily 
    create, organize, and manage your tasks within the terminal. 

Actions
    add
    remove
    change
    complete

Options
    -h | Show help page  


Examples:
    tba
